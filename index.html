About OXIDRA

Innovation in Medical Delivery

OXIDRA s.r.o. is developing a breakthrough dual-chamber aerosol spray that combines wound cleaning and hemostasis (bleeding cessation) in a single, easy-to-use product. Our innovation transforms Traumacel, a proven hemostatic agent, into a sprayable aerosol form using ecological nitrogen propellants and cutting-edge bag-on-valve (BOV) technology.

We are committed to advancing wound care across healthcare facilities, emergency services, veterinary clinics, and consumer first-aid applications.

Why OXIDRA?

  • ✓ Dual-Function: Clean and stop bleeding in one spray
  • ✓ Eco-Friendly: Nitrogen propellant, no harmful chemicals
  • ✓ Easy to Use: No mixing, no training required
  • ✓ Fast Acting: Immediate hemostatic effect
  • ✓ Scalable: From hospitals to home first-aid kits

The Problem & Our Solution

Traditional Hemostatics

  • Powder-based, messy application
  • Requires direct contact with wound
  • Multiple products needed
  • Difficult to use in emergency settings
  • Chemical propellants (CFCs, HFCs)
  • Longer learning curve
  • Limited shelf life

OXIDRA Spray

  • Clean, controlled spray application
  • Reaches difficult-to-access wounds
  • All-in-one solution
  • Intuitive, immediate use
  • Ecological nitrogen propellant
  • Minimal training required
  • Extended shelf life

Our Technology

🎯

Dual-Chamber Design

Separate chambers for wound cleansing and hemostatic agent ensure optimal delivery and maximum efficacy with zero cross-contamination.

🌱

Ecological Propellant

Nitrogen-based propellant eliminates harmful CFCs and HFCs. Environmentally responsible without compromising performance.

⚡

Bag-on-Valve (BOV)

Advanced BOV system ensures consistent spray pattern, precise dosing, and reliable performance from first use to last drop.
